About Us

Team Vrukshavista

We are Team Vrukshavista from Panjab University,UIET Chandigarh. We are here IES Bhopal, to participate in the prestigious Smart India Hackathon. Our team involves our leader , and the members are- Front developers as Vishal Kumar, Avishi Jain, Anurag Kanswal ML/AI leads as Reek Chatterjee and Abhijit Singh and the backend developer Dhruv Bhatt

Our Objectives :

  • Image Data Analysis:

    Develop a computer vision algorithm for analyzing satellite imagery or aerial photographs to accurately detect and identify trees within designated forest areas. The algorithm should account for variations in tree species, sizes, and environmental conditions for reliable results.

  • Tree Counting and Categorization:

    Design a system to count the number of trees in the specified area and categorize them based on species or relevant parameters like diameter (girth). The solution aims to provide accurate and detailed information about the tree population to support decision-making during land diversion projects.

  • Accuracy and Validation:

    Implement mechanisms to validate the accuracy of the image analytics solution by comparing results with ground-truth data obtained through manual surveys or reliable methods. The objective is to achieve a high level of accuracy and minimize false positives or false negatives in tree identification and counting.

  • Scalability and Efficiency:

    Develop an efficient and scalable solution capable of processing large volumes of image data within a reasonable timeframe. Consider optimization techniques and parallel processing approaches to ensure timely results, especially for large forest areas or time-sensitive projects.

  • Integration and Visualization:

    Enable seamless integration of the image analytics solution with existing forest management systems or tools. Provide visualizations and interactive interfaces for users to explore and interpret results easily.
